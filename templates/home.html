Process
DAO sprouting is performed in three major phases: Registration, Listing, and Funding.
Each phase contains three steps.

 Note: This dapp uses .
Upon logging into Meta-Mask, your accounts will be available to the dapp.
The account you have active at any given moment will be used by the dapp.

Registration

#1 - Submit

Submit DAO information to be registered. Expect to receive a notification if the submitted DAO information requires additional clarification or when it is approved. Upon approval, it will show up in the registry with a status of 'Verifying'.

#2 - Verify

Verifiers verify multiple objective data points and receive tokens for proof of work. Profile of the project is generated.

#3 - Rate

After an initial comment period, the project is approved and community members can rate it on its more subjective merits.

Listing

#4 - Rank

As DAOs are rated, they get ranked on multiple parameters each with its own list.

#5 - Vote

DAOs are voted on to determine which get funded and how much.

#6 - Fund

Those that win in the voting receive funds per milestone via smart contract.

Funding

#7 - Prototype Milestone

Funded DAOs receive enough funds to fund its prototype.

#8 - GA Milestone

Successful prototyped DAOs receive enough funds to fund their General Availability.

#9 - Marketing Milestone

Those funded DAOs make a successful GA release, receive funds to market their DAO.
