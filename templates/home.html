Registration Process
This dapp uses .
Upon logging into Meta-Mask, your accounts will be available to the dapp.
The account you have active at any given moment will be used by the dapp.

#1 - Purchase

Purchase DST tokens with Ether.

#2 - Verify

Verify multiple objective data points and receive tokens for proof of work. Profile of the project is generated.

#3 - Register

After an initial comment period, the project is registered and community members can rate it on its more subjective merits.
